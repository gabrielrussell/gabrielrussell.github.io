My 11 year old son was pondering irrational numbers. And was asking how if π was random why does everyone have the same value. I asked Claude to bang this out so that we could look at the implementation.

It takes a parameter "n" specifying how many digits to generate. It takes about 15 seconds to generate ten million digits of pi.

It's running at [wiccatech.com/wasmpi](/wasmpi/index.html?n=100000) and the source is at
[GitHub](https://github.com/gabrielrussell/wasmpi).
